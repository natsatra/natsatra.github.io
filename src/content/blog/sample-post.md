An opening paragraph before the first heading. Set up what the post is about and why it matters.

## Background

The context a reader needs before the main content.

## Main section

The core of the post. Split into more `##` sections as needed.

### Sub-point

Supporting detail, examples, or code.

## Takeaways

What the reader should remember or do next.
